Recycl.AI classes explained

Recycl.AI is trained to recongise three main classes; Deposit recycling, Regualar recycling, and Flexible plastic. These classes contain most of the common items that are often not disposed off properly.

Flexible Plastics

Flexible plastic is a type of plastic that can be easily bent or folded without breaking. This category includes materials like plastic bags, shrink wrap, candy wrappers and flexible packaging

When flexible plastic is recycled, it undergoes a process where it is collected, sorted, cleaned, and melted into pellets. The pellets which can then be used to manufacture a variety of items, such as new bags, containers, or even synthetic clothes.
